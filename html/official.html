Members of Cabinet

Each Member of Cabinet is appointed by the Premier. All Members of Cabinet are given the title Ministers; whom are party representatives within the Legislature. Under our Constitution, Cabinet consists of the Premier and a maximum of twelve Ministers from the House of Assembly. A minimum of one Minister has to be appointed from the Senate; or a maximum of two. The minimum amount of Cabinet Ministers is seven, including the Premier.
